Multilingual

This type of sentiment analysis is more complex than others because it requires many preprocessing steps and online resources.

Emotion recognition

This is an advanced form of sentiment analysis that identifies emotions through physical actions, facial expressions, speaking, behavior, writing, and gestures.

Intent-based

This type of sentiment analysis aims to understand why customers, employees, or other parties act the way they do.

Sarcasm analysis

This is a critical area of focus for sentiment analysis systems because sarcasm and irony are so common in everyday conversation.

Subjectivity classification

TThis involves classifying sentences into subjective and objective classes. Subjective sentences express the writer's opinions, evaluations, emotions, beliefs, and judgments.
